About EmbodX

At EmbodX, we are on a mission to bring general-purpose robotics out of the lab and into the real world. Born from cutting-edge research at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), we are bridging the gap between human intent and robotic action.

We believe the future of automation isn't about replacing people—it's about empowering them. By lowering the technical barriers to entry, we are enabling a new generation of manufacturers to deploy sophisticated robotics without writing a single line of code.

Our Approach: The Data Flywheel

The greatest hurdle in modern robotics is the "physical data gap." While AI has mastered language by reading the entire internet, robots lack a comparable library of physical experiences to learn from.

EmbodX is solving this by creating a Data Flywheel for physical intelligence:

01

Democratize Access

We provide an intuitive, AR-powered interface that allows anyone to "sketch" robot trajectories in the real world. By making programming as easy as showing the robot what to do, we unlock automation for SMEs and non-experts.

02

Capture Real-World Skills

As more users deploy our tools to solve diverse manufacturing challenges, we capture high-quality, real-world manipulation data that currently doesn't exist.

03

Train Foundation Models

This data fuels the training of our Physical Foundation Models—AI that understands physics, context, and movement. These models feed back into our system, making our robots smarter, more adaptable, and easier to use with every interaction.

What We Do

We replace complex coding environments with intuitive Imitation Learning. Using our augmented reality (AR) interface, operators can visualize and guide robot movements directly in their physical workspace.

This "no-code" approach removes the guesswork from automation. It allows general-purpose robots to be reprogrammed for new tasks in minutes rather than weeks, transforming idle hardware into flexible, productive teammates.

Our Vision

We don't believe in "dark factories" devoid of humans. We believe in a future where humans and robots work side-by-side. EmbodX is building the infrastructure to make this collaboration seamless, safe, and accessible to everyone.
